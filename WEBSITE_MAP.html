Pages at a glance

Each card shows what the page is for, which components it uses, and the client review risk attached to that page.

/

Homepage. Uses Nav, Hero, Marquee, Philosophy, Services, Signature, Experience, Editorial, Testimonials, Location, CTA, Footer.

Review: brand claims, credibility copy, Location `#book` anchor.

/services

Service index. Uses Nav, PageHeader, Reveal, Footer, `services` data, image/link components.

Links: every card goes to `/services/{slug}`.

/services/[slug]

Service detail template. Uses Nav, Reveal, Footer, `services` data, related services, booking CTAs.

Review: service wording and claims should be approved.

/consultation

Consultation explainer page. Uses Nav, Reveal, Footer, image/link components.

Review: physician-led, 30 min, photo documentation, cost ranges, cancellation policy.

/results

Results, stats, quotes, before/after, gallery. Uses Nav, PageHeader, Reveal, BeforeAfter, Footer.

Review: highest-risk claims page. Confirm every number, quote, and image claim.

/contact

Contact info, InquiryForm, and embedded Google map. Uses Nav, InquiryForm, Footer.

Review: one-business-day reply promise and hours.

/book

Service appointment form. Uses Nav, BookingForm, Footer, service query validation.

Review: confirmed 30-minute booking and no-charge copy.

/book/consultation

Consultation booking form. Uses Nav, ConsultationForm, Footer.

Review: 30-minute, no treatment same day, photo-documented assessment.

Similar patterns

These are not necessarily the same component, but they behave or look similar. This is the part that was hard to follow in the Markdown map.

Large hero systems

`Hero` is reused only on `/`, but similar hero layouts are hand-coded on `/consultation`, `/services/[slug]`, `/contact`, `/book`, and `/book/consultation`.

Service card systems

Service cards exist in `components/Services.jsx`, `/services/page.jsx`, and the related-services section of `/services/[slug]`.

CTA bands

`CTA` is homepage-only. Dark CTA bands are separately coded in `/consultation`, `/results`, and `/services/[slug]`.

Protocol cards

Card grids appear in `Experience`, `/consultation`, and `/services/[slug]`; each uses local or service-specific data.

Contact/map blocks

`Location` appears on `/`. `/contact` separately codes the contact details, inquiry form, and Google map iframe.

Testimonials/quotes

`Testimonials` appears on `/`. `/results` has its own local quote array and stats, not the same component.

Data and actions

This shows where shared data and server actions flow. The service data is the main reuse driver across the site.

lib/services.js

Used by Nav, homepage Services, services index, service details, BookingForm, `/book`, and `app/actions.js`.

Fields: no, slug, name, blurb, summary, time, modality, img, tags, facts, story, goodFor, boundaries, process, faqs.

submitBooking

Server action in `app/actions.js`. Called by `BookingForm` and `ConsultationForm`.

Form→submitBooking→email/logging

submitInquiry

Server action in `app/actions.js`. Called by `InquiryForm` on `/contact`.

InquiryForm→submitInquiry→email/logging

Client review flags

Use this as the meeting checklist. These are the areas most likely to need approval, proof, or toned-down wording.

  • Results statsConfirm treatment count, review count, referral rate, and serving-since date.
  • TestimonialsConfirm quotes, initials, treatment labels, patient consent, and whether they are real.
  • Before/after pageCurrent before/after uses the same image. Clinical/photo-release claims need confirmation.
  • Provider claimsConfirm "physician-led", "European-trained", and any provider-specific claims.
  • Booking promisesConfirm 30-minute appointments, no charge until visit, no treatment on consultation day, and cancellation policy.
  • Hours and contactHours appear in Footer, Contact, and Location. Confirm they match the clinic exactly.
  • Response timeContact page and form promise one-business-day response.
  • Placeholder linksPrivacy, Terms, and Instagram are currently `#` links.
  • Mobile navigationNo full mobile menu exists yet; confirm if logo + Book button is enough.
  • Homepage anchor`Location` links to `#book`, but there is no matching `id="book"` element.
  • Service detail copyNew service pages are educational and cautious, but the client should approve wording.
  • MetadataSome SEO metadata still contains strong positioning claims; review before launch.
