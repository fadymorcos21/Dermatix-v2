[ 01 ] — Philosophy

A studio, not a clinic. A protocol, not a product.

We borrow rigor from medicine and grace from interior design.

Dermatix exists for the diminishing returns no one talks about — the moment when “good” skin needs the eye of a specialist to become extraordinary. We start with diagnosis, not menus.

— Clinically led

Every protocol is designed and supervised by a board-certified dermatologist. No rotating staff, no upsell scripts.

— Evidence-first

We ignore trends. Every device, peel and active in the studio is FDA-cleared and tracked over twelve months of patient outcomes.

— Designed in

Suites by Studio Mellor — wide oak floors, daylight from above, and a single chair. No waiting room televisions.

— Quietly priced

We publish every price, every minute, every active. Members keep one card on file; statements arrive once a quarter.

How a visit actually goes.

No upsell scripts. No mystery additions on the receipt.

01
Diagnostic — 20m

A board-certified dermatologist reviews your skin under VISIA imaging and writes the protocol you'll receive in your portal that evening.

02
Treatment — 30 to 90m

A single specialist, in a single suite, delivering a single agreed-upon plan. The price you saw is the price you pay.
